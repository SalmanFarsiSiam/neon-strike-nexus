About MPA Community

Learn about our mission, history, and the team behind the MPA Launcher.

Our Mission

The MPA (Multiplayer Association) Community is dedicated to preserving and enhancing the multiplayer experience of IGI 2: Covert Strike. Our mission is to maintain an active community of players, provide stable and secure servers, and continue developing new features and content for this classic game.

We believe that great games deserve to live on, and we're committed to keeping IGI 2 multiplayer alive and thriving for both veteran players and newcomers alike.

Our History

The MPA Community was founded in 2018 by a group of dedicated IGI 2 players who wanted to revive the game's multiplayer mode after official support ended. What began as a small Discord server and a basic launcher has grown into a thriving community with thousands of active players.

Over the years, we've developed multiple versions of the MPA Launcher, each introducing new features, improvements, and compatibility updates. We've created custom maps, hosted tournaments, and built a friendly, supportive community of players from around the world.

From our humble beginnings to today, our passion for IGI 2 multiplayer has never wavered, and we continue to grow and evolve with each passing year.

Join Our Community

We're more than just a gaming community—we're a family of IGI 2 enthusiasts who share a passion for this classic game. Whether you're a veteran player from the game's original release or new to IGI 2, we welcome you to join our community.

Connect with us through our Discord server, participate in community events, share your feedback, or even contribute to the development of MPA Launcher. Every member of our community plays a valuable role in keeping IGI 2 multiplayer alive and thriving.
